01 / About

Behind the lens

Jaipur, Rajasthan
In The Moment
Simarpreet Singh

I'm a professional photographer based in Jaipur, specialising in the spaces where energy is raw and unfiltered — concerts, dance performances, DJ events, music sessions, and sports.

My work is about presence. Being there at the right moment, anticipating what's about to happen before it does. Every frame is a decision — light, timing, emotion.

Whether it's the peak of a jump, the blur of a spin, or the silence between two beats — I find the moment that lives forever.

Simarpreet
